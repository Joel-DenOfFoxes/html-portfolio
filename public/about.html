About Me

Hi there, my name is Joel Jeffery and I am the founder and owner of Den of Foxes. Den of Foxes is a creative brand agency based in Sydney, Australia. We work with brands to push creative boundaries through our comprehensive services, including brand identity design, creative development, web design, social media, and now Generative AI. Our philosophy is that great design should not only improve the visual presence of a business, but that it should also aid in communicating the ideals a brand upholds - resulting in clever and sophisticated work that tells visual stories and solves client problems.
